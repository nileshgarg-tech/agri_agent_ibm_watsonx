# Usage Examples

## LOG - Recording Farm Activities

```
"I just sold 50 pounds of tomatoes to the market for $75"
→ ✅ Logged: Sale of tomatoes.

"Harvested 100 pounds of potatoes from the west field"
→ ✅ Logged: Harvest of potatoes.

"Spent $45 on tractor fuel today"
→ ✅ Logged: Expense of tractor fuel.

"Bought 5 bags of fertilizer for $120"
→ ✅ Logged: Purchase of fertilizer.
```

## QUERY - Asking About Data

```
"How much did I make from tomato sales last month?"
→ Returns total sales from tomatoes in logs

"What was my biggest expense this week?"
→ Finds and reports highest expense entry

"How many pounds of potatoes did I harvest total?"
→ Sums up all potato harvest quantities

"Show me all purchases from last week"
→ Lists relevant purchase entries
```

## REPORT - Getting Summaries

```
"Give me a sales report for this week"
→ Formatted summary of all sales with totals

"Generate an expense report for last month"
→ Breakdown of expenses by category

"Show me a profit and loss statement"
→ Income vs expenses analysis

"Summarize all my activities from yesterday"
→ Overview of all logged activities
```

## GENERAL - Farming Advice

```
"What's the best time to plant garlic?"
→ General farming advice

"How do I prevent potato blight?"
→ Disease prevention information

"What's a good crop rotation for tomatoes?"
→ Agricultural best practices

"Hello, how are you?"
→ Conversational response
```

## Testing the System

1. Start with logging a few activities
2. Try querying specific information
3. Generate a report to see all data
4. Ask general farming questions

Example session:
```
You: I sold 30 pounds of carrots for $50
Bot: ✅ Logged: Sale of carrots.

You: I harvested 75 pounds of tomatoes
Bot: ✅ Logged: Harvest of tomatoes.

You: How much did I make from carrot sales?
Bot: Based on your logs, you made $50 from carrot sales.

You: Give me a summary of today's activities
Bot: Today's Activities Summary:
     - Sale: 30 lbs carrots ($50)
     - Harvest: 75 lbs tomatoes
```
